--- layout: post title: tomcat报PermGen space错误，如何解决(window)? category: 服务器 tomcat description: tomcat报PermGen space错误，如何解决(window)? img: no.png keywords: tomcat,PermGen space ---

修改TOMCAT_HOME/bin/catalina.bat，在“echo "Using CATALINA_BASE: $CATALINA_BASE"”上面加入以下行 


@set JAVA_OPTS=%JAVA_OPTS% -server -XX:PermSize=128M -XX:MaxPermSize=512M
